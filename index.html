Projects

I've worked on some neat things.
ReCAST for LCCPW

My Capstone group is working on a project called ReCAST for Lenfest Center for Community Workforce Partnerships. We will be building their system that takes applicants and turns them into partners! These partners will be able to use our system to file invoices and recieve status notifications of their process, as well as be able to reach out and request further funding.

OverCoach

OverCoach is a personal project that I am building. This project is meant to help coaches who are in the Overwatch community with their daily duties to their team. A couple features that will be included are a team organizer, map top-down editor, looking for group bulletin, and character stat sheets!

About Me

In my free time I love playing games with my friends online. One year we decided as a group to make a team and join some tournaments! We quickly found out that we are pretty good at the game Overwatch, and we started winning more and more. Eventually we ended up creating the club team here at Temple!
